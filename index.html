Body Alphabet Mapping

Each letter is mapped to specific locations on the body:

A: Right Collarbone
B: Left Collarbone
C: Right Below A
D: Left Below B

The pattern continues down the body with letters alternating sides.

Numbers 1-26 are mapped to the back of the body in a similar pattern.

Brain Language Construction

Each word in brain language follows a specific construction pattern:

[Brain Region] + [Letter 1 Position] + [Letter 2 Position] + ... + [Letter N Position] + [Deflate]

For example, "clap" stored in the right back side of the brain would be constructed as:

RBS + C + L + A + P + DEFLATE
